I am a Doctoral Student at the University of Southern California, working with Lu Zhang, Sisi Wang, and Hung Chak Derrick Ho (CityU.HK). I use machine learning and spatial statistics to analyze earth observation data (AI4EO) to support sustainable development. Before joining USC, I was a Research Assistant at The University of Hong Kong, working with Jason Chun Shing Pun. I obtained my undergraduate degree at Sun Yat-Sen University, working with Qian Shi.
